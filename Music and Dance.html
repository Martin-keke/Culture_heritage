    beautiful traditional dances

    1. Mokhibo
    2. Mohobelo
    3. Litolobonya
    4. Ntlamu
    5. Selialia

    Mokhibo is a dance that women wear big dresses and kneel down and move their shoulders

    Mohobelo is a dance that men use sticks and raise their stick and feet in a slow pace

    Litolobonya is a dance that women wear a dress made of shreds and bottle caps and move their waist to dance

    Ntlamu is a dance that men use beads on the legs and move the legs up and down in a fast motion

    Selialia is a dance that women and men dance together where women move body half circular motion while rising their hands up to their shoulders and elbow a lit little bit bend and men dance besides them
